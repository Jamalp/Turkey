Kapadokya

Cities built into mountains & Fairy Chimneys

Fairy Chimneys

Cappadocia, or Kapadokya is probably one of the coolest landscapes ever. It looks like you're in a desert on the moon, for real! There are hills, mountains & caves everywhere. Small homes that were carved into the sides of cliffs and mountains from THOUSANDS of years ago can be found everywhere for you to crawl in for some shade if you want. Be honest, is your mind blown?

At night, it gets so dark out that you can barely see in front of you if you're not near a town or city. We rode from Göreme to Uçhisar on our moped and once you get out of the "city" area, its pitch black. Imagine, complete darkness besides the light from our moped illuminating the street just barely in front of us. The howling wind and scattered noises of who knows what kind of animals, and way off in the distance the bright lights of a far off city built right into a towering mountainside. It felt like I was in a post apocalyptic world searching for gas and food or something. I wish you could see it.
